## Privacy & Analytics
This website exists to share information about the Eagle Quarter redevelopment
in Newbury and its impact on the local community.

Your privacy matters, and this site is intentionally built to collect as little
data as possible.
### What we collect
We collect **anonymous, aggregated usage data** to understand how the site is
used. This includes things like:
- Page views
- Referring websites (for example, Google search)
- Approximate location (city / country level only)
- Device and browser type

This data **cannot be used to identify you personally**.
### Cookies
This site does **not** use cookies for analytics or tracking.

There is no advertising, remarketing, or cross-site tracking.
### Analytics tools used
- **Google Analytics (GA4)**  
  Configured in *cookieless mode* using Google Consent Mode. No cookies are set
  and no personal identifiers are stored.
- **Cloudflare Web Analytics**  
  A privacy-first analytics service provided by Cloudflare. It does not use
  cookies and does not track users across websites.

These tools help improve content and site performance while respecting user
privacy.
### External links
Some pages link to third-party websites (for example, local news or council
documents). This site is not responsible for the privacy practices of external
sites.
