Newbury benefits from direct access to the A339 and A4 road corridors, and Newbury railway station provides regular services towards Reading, London Paddington and other destinations. This connectivity underscores the potential of town-centre redevelopment to attract residents, businesses, and visitors.
### Urban Context
The Eagle Quarter/Old Town site sits within the Newbury Town Centre Conservation Area, characterised by a mix of historic streets, listed buildings, and civic spaces. This urban setting has shaped planning discussions regarding the scale and design of redevelopment proposals.
### Local Amenities
